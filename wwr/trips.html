Trip Options

Blues Crews

Float easy with just enough splash to keep things interesting. This laid-back trip is perfect for first-timers or folks who prefer scenery over screams. Think calm water, sunshine, and a few giggle-worthy rapids to keep it fun.

Call Home

Not too wild, not too mild—this one rides the sweet spot. You’ll tackle lively rapids, twist through tight bends, and maybe shout something that makes the guides laugh. It's enough action to feel brave, but not enough to scare your group chat.

Call Mom

This trip brings the chaos. Expect crashing waves, sudden drops, and at least one moment where you lose your paddle—and your composure. It’s safe, but your mom wouldn’t think so.

Mainly Swim

Big water, bigger energy. You’ll get bucked, drenched, and possibly ejected (don’t worry, we’ve got you). This trip is for thrill seekers who measure success in bruises and stories. Not recommended for dry personalities.

Jurassic Ride

A cinematic plunge through misty cliffs and ancient boulders. It’s dramatic, it’s loud, it’s probably the coolest thing you’ll do all summer. Dinosaurs not included, but adrenaline definitely is.
