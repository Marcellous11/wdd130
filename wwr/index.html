Safety

Safety comes first on every trip we run. Our trained guides, top-quality gear, and clear instructions help ensure every guest has a safe, fun time on the river.

Safety Highlights:

  • Coast Guard-approved life jackets and helmets for all guests
  • Experienced, certified guides in every raft
  • Regularly inspected and well-maintained equipment
